Hot or Cold

A Number Guessing Game

A random number is generated with each game, between 1 and 100.

Guess the number unti you find it!

You'll get feedback to let you know if you need to go higher or lower.

Previous guesses will be displayed. A red background around the guess means it was lower than the number. A blue background means it was higher.

Try to get the number in the fewest number of guesses!
